Dehaka

Primal Pack Leader

On Zerus, the zerg pack leader Dehaka was
a constantly evolving apex predator.
His drive to slay the strongest prey and consume their
Essence has now led him to the Nexus, where Dehaka
stalks his opponents across the battlefield.
Able to regenerate through massive damage
and swiftly move into and out of combat,
Dehaka uses the primal zerg's mutability
to deadly effect.
