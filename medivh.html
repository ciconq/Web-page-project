Medivh

The Last Guardian

Under the influence of the demon lord Sargeras,
Medivh opened the Dark Portal and unleashed
the orcish Horde upon Azeroth.
To atone for this sin, he rallied a new generation of heroes
to stand against the Burning Legion during the Third War.
In the Nexus, Medivh continues to lead his allies to greatness.
He observes the battle from up high in Raven Form,
then swoops down and uses shields and portals
to turn the tide of battle.
Now, he takes his place amongst the legends.
